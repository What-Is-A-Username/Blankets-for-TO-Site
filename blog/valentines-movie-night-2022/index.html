Valentine's Movie Night

Events

Online

by William Huang

February 21st, 2022

To celebrate Valentine's Day 2022 with members of the Blankets for T.O. community, we organized a virtual movie night on Friday February 18th, 2022, to watch the romantic comedy film Crazy Rich Asians. It was open to everyone and was also an opportunity to introduce our group to attendees and connect with other members of the community.

Additionally, attendees were entered into a giveaway which was made possible by student community funding from the University of Toronto Scarborough. Thank you for helping to make these student events possible!

Blankets for T.O. would like to thank everyone that turned out. It was amazing to talk with our members as well as to introduce our organization's goals to attendees.

Stay updated about future events and initiatives via our social media, our website and our mailing list.
