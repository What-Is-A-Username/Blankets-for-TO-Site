Meet and Greet January 2020

January 29th, 2020

BTO successfully hosted a meet and greet at the University of Toronto Scarborough for students to introduce BTO to the community. First of all, thank you to all those students that were able to take the time to come! It was amazing to see and meet all of you. The agenda of the session was to inform students about how they could contribute to BTO, including how they could become a member, apply for more senior positions, donate to the cause, and become a volunteer at future events.
