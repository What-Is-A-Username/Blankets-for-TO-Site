October 2020

Events

Online

by Blankets for T.O. on October 31st, 2020

Estimated reading time: 1 minute

Blankets for T.O. hosted two events for October 2020!

We started off the Fall semester by organizing an open giveaway for a $20 Amazon gift card and Blankets for T.O. membership.

In the spirit of Halloween, we hosted a pumpkin carving competition open to all UTSC students! It was also a good time to destress from midterm exams, since students were able to socialize and have a good time while competing to carve the best jack-o'-lantern they could. The pumpkins carved were awesome! Thanks to all those who participated!
