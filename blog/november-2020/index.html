    November Events

    November 30th, 2020

    November 2020 at BTO was highlighted by two initiatives we were able to complete amid the difficult circumstances posed by the COVID-19 Pandemic.

    We completed a food distribution initiative this month to distribute 10 packs of 60 donuts to the homeless across Toronto. One of our stops was to a homeless shelter at the Warden Woods Community Centre.

    To help out local small businesses during the hardships of the COVID-19 pandemic, we decided to help promote an example of one through our social media! Members of BTO worked with the owner of Nasir's Gourmet Hot Dog Stand to take a professional photo shoot and make posters to promote their business!

    Water.org UTSC x BTO Winter/Holiday Event

    December 1st, 2020

    We collaborate with Water.org UTSC to provide students with opportunities to socialize with friends, win prizes in games and raffles, and become better informed about homelessness in Toronto and water scarcity around the world.
