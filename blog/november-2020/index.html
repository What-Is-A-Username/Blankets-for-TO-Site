Campaigns

by , November 30th, 2020

November 2020 at BTO was highlighted by two initiatives we were able to complete amid the difficult circumstances posed by the COVID-19 Pandemic.

Donation to a Homeless Shelter

We completed a food distribution initiative this month to distribute 10 packs of 60 donuts to the homeless across Toronto. One of our stops was to a homeless shelter at the Warden Woods Community Centre.

Our Day at the Hot Dog Stand

To help out local small businesses during the hardships of the COVID-19 pandemic, we decided to help promote an example of one through our social media! Blankets for T. O. worked with the owner of Nasir's Gourmet Hot Dog Stand, a food stand located right at the front of the UTSC campus.

Now that nearly all university classes have went online, his business has been struggling immensely with the lack of foot traffic. Blankets for T.O spent the day to take a photo shoot and to help promote his business. A video showcasing his business and his awesome food was uploaded to our TikTok, immediately getting traffic and likes! View the video here.

Be sure to check his food out when you get the chance to. Let's show our support to local families and small businesses!
