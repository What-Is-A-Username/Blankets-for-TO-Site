Blankets for T.O. General Meeting Fall 2021

Announcements

Events

Online

by William Huang

September 27th, 2021

Blankets for T.O. held its annual general meeting on Thursday September 16th, 2021 from 7pm to 8pm over Zoom! We introduced ourselves to the UTSC student community and laid out our plans for the 2021-2022 school year. It was amazing to see the turnout and it was especially encouraging to see how many first year students were interested in our cause!

Need a refresher on what we discussed? Or did you miss it? Dont worry, read below for a short summary!

Who is Blankets for T.O.?

Founded in 2019, Blankets for T.O. is a non-profit organization at UTSC providing support and resources to those in need of housing around the Greater Toronto Area. We advocate for ending the stigma associated with homelessness and breaking down barriers preventing their reintegration into society, by working with shelters, Members of Parliament, professors, and local organizations.

How can I get involved with Blankets for T.O.?

Joining the Blankets for T.O. cause gives everyone a chance to give back to and be part of the community to make a difference. It is also an opportunity to develop your skills, make new friendships, and build your confidence.

Start with viewing and following us on social media (links here)! Consider signing up for our club membership for 2021-2022, since members are offered exclusive access to discounts from our sponsors, raffles and giveaways. We will also be sharing opportunities to volunteer with us during the 2021-2022 school year.

This year we plan on offering volunteer opportunities and co-curricular record recognition to our members! More information will be coming out later this semester.

We also accepted applications for two executive positions: First Year Representative and Graphic Designer. Applications closed on September 20th. Thanks to all those applied! We are currently going over applications and we will introduce our new executive members soon.

Giveaway and Scavenger Hunt

We held a raffle draw for an Amazon gift card for attendees of our general meeting! Participants earned tickets for their participation in the meeting and interactions over social media. Congratulations to Ming Ming Gong for winning the draw!

Try our Scavenger Hunt activity made by our Vice President of Technology, William Huang, where you can show off your knowledge of Blankets for T.O. and of our site! Although you will no longer receive raffle tickets for participating, see how much you know about us!

From our executive team: thank you!

Thank you so much to those who came out to our meeting! We were excited to see so many motivated students, especially first year students, in attendance. Many were eager to know how they could make donations themselves and how they could get involved with our events going forward.

Have any questions for us? See here for links to contact us over social media or email.
