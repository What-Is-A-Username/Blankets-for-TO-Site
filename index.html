Our Organization

Blankets for T.O. is a non-profit organization situated at the University of Toronto that strives to provide support and resources to those in need within the city of Toronto, as well as raise awareness on the ongoing stigma surrounding homelessness, with the ultimate goal of eradicating it. So far, our team has worked towards our goal by distributing food blankets, and masks to those in need, as well as hand sanitizers due to the ongoing pandemic. We have also interviewed and shared the experiences through our social media presences of those who support the less fortunate, in the hopes of educating others on the severity of homelessness in Toronto, and encouraging them to support the people in their community.
